Ventox Rules

Here are the rules and expectations that are in place for all members of the Ventox community.

Discord & Server Rules

Level 1 Punishments

» 1st Offence - Formal Warning » 2nd Offence - 1 Hour Temporary Mute » 3rd Offence - 1 Day Temporary Mute » 4th Offence - 1 Day Temporary Ban » 5th Offence - 1 Week Temporary Ban

  • Asking about Appeals/Applications
  • Disrespect

    Defined as comments that go beyond the nature of a "normal" toxicity Includes homophobic comments Includes harassment (at staff discretion)

  • English in Chat

    All conversations in Public Chat must be in English

  • Links in Chat

    Defined as any non-Ventox official links in Public Chat

  • Misleading Staff

    Defined as purposely wasting staff's time Defined as attempting to bypass rules in an effort to get a reaction from staff

  • Racism

    Defined as any racist/hate speech-based comments about a religious group, ethnic group, or anything likewise

  • Sexual Comments

    Defined as comments that go beyond the nature of a "normal" conversation

  • Spam

    Defined as 5 or more of the same/similar messages within 15 seconds - Reports/proof must include message timestamps Defined as a single user taking up the majority (75%+) of the chat box Includes any functionality which results in a chat message (i.e. Faction Relations, Private Messages [when reported])
