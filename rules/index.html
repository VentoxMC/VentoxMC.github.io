Ventox Rules

Here are the rules and expectations that are in place for all members of the Ventox community.

Discord & Server Rules

Level 1

Punishments

  • 1st Offence - Formal Warning
  • 2nd Offence - 1 Hour Temporary Mute
  • 3rd Offence - 1 Day Temporary Mute
  • 4th Offence - 1 Day Temporary Ban
  • 5th Offence - 1 Week Temporary Ban

Offences

  • Asking about Appeals/Applications

  • Disrespect

    • Defined as comments that go beyond the nature of a "normal" toxicity
    • Includes homophobic comments
    • Includes harassment (at staff discretion)

  • English in Chat

    • All conversations in Public Chat must be in English

  • Links in Chat

    • Defined as any non-Ventox official links in Public Chat

  • Misleading Staff

    • Defined as purposely wasting staff's time
    • Defined as attempting to bypass rules in an effort to get a reaction from staff

  • Racism

    • Defined as any racist/hate speech-based comments about a religious group, ethnic group, or anything likewise

  • Sexual Comments

    • Defined as comments that go beyond the nature of a "normal" conversation

  • Spam

    • Defined as 5 or more of the same/similar messages within 15 seconds
    • Defined as a single user taking up the majority (75%+) of the chat box
    • Includes any functionality which results in a chat message (i.e. Team Relations, Private Messages [when reported])

Level 2

Punishments

  • 1st Offence - 1 Day Temporary Mute
  • 2nd Offence - 1 Week Temporary Mute
  • 3rd Offence - 2 Week Temporary Mute
  • 4th Offence - Permanent Mute

Offences

  • Advertisement

    • Defined as any message which has the intent to steal players from the network
    • "Intent to Steal" can include directly asking people to join another server
    • "Intent to Steal" can include posting an IP to another website

  • Death Wishes/Death Threats

    • Defined as any message which wishes death upon another player

  • Inappropriate Links

    • Defined as any link which leads to "inappropriate" websites
    • Includes links to pornographic websites, gambling websites, etc.

Level 3

Punishments

  • 1st Offence - 1 Week Temporary Ban
  • 2nd Offence - 30 Day Temporary Ban
  • 3rd Offence - 90 Day Temporary Ban
  • 4th Offence - Permanent Ban

Offences

  • Automation Blacklisted Modifications

  • Blackmail

    • Defined as threatening to use proof against someone in return for personal gain (i.e. IRL money, base coordinates, etc)

  • Distasteful Builds

    • Defined as any build which would break another rule (i.e. racism)

  • DDoS/Dox Threats

    • Defined as threatening to DDoS/Dox any corporation (i.e. players, the server, the store, any third-party entity)

  • Forging Proof

    • Defined as modifying proof to incriminate someone else

  • IRL Trade

    • Defined as advertising/engaging in selling in-game items for IRL Money (i.e. wire transfer, paypal, etc.)
    • Defined as advertising/engaging in trading in-game items for actions in other games (i.e. CSGO knife)

  • Teaming with Hackers

    • Defined as assisting obvious hackers in PvP (i.e. giving health potions/golden apples to obvious B-Hoppers)
    • Defined as funding obvious hackers with wealth (i.e. giving Faction wealth after witnessing them Fly)

  • Witholding Proof

    • Defined as submitting a report with proof from 7+ days ago

Level 4

Punishments

  • 1st Offence - 30 Day Temporary Ban
  • 2nd Offence - 90 Day Temporary Ban
  • 3rd Offence - Permanent Ban

Offences

  • Blacklisted Modifications

    • Includes the use of any hacked client or illegal mod (some exceptions provided under "Automation Blacklisted Modifications" under Level 3 Punishments)
    • Includes the use of Printer

  • Exploiting Glitches

    • Includes the use of bugs for personal gain

  • Griefing

    • Includes the griefing of any protected space
    • Includes lava/water casting anywhere in the world

  • Macros/Scripts

    • Includes any automated commands, macros, or scripts

Level 5

Punishments

  • 1st Offence - Permanent Ban

Offences

  • Chargeback

    • Any appeals regarding Chargebacks should be dealt with through Stores Issues

  • Inappropriate Username/Skin/Cape

    • Defined as any character cosmetic which would break another rule
    • Any appeals can be accepted once the player changes their username, skin, or cape

Level 6

Punishments

  • 1st Offence - Permanent Ban*

* appealable after 6 months

Offences

  • DDoS/Dox

    • Defined as any confirmed cases of DDoS/Doxing, which can be linked back to a specific player

  • Exploiting Dupes

    • Includes the use of dupes for personal gain

  • Malicious Links

    • Defined as any link which leads to "malicious" websites
    • Includes links to viruses, phishing websites, spam websites

  • Releasing Exploit Information

    • Defined as any publication of information regarding how glitches/bugs/dupes work which provide personal gain

  • Releasing Personal Information

    • Defined as any publication of another player's legal name (middle & last), address, nearby points of interest, previous education centers
    • Defined as any specific place of employment (i.e. stating you work at Walmart is not specific)

Exclusive Punishments

Admission

  • 1st Offence - The Next Punishment for the Rule Admitted to have been Broken

  • Admitting to Breaking a Rule

    • Defined as admitting to breaking any Non-Immediately Appealable Offence via Public Chat
    • Defined as admitting to breaking any Bannable Offence via Private Messages

Evasion

  • Original Punishment was Temporary - Double the duration of the original punishment on all impacted accounts

  • Original Punishment was Permanent - Permanently punish the new account for evasion of the original account

  • Evasion/bypassing a Punishment

    • Defined as logging onto the server while banned on another account linked to your IP
    • Defined as logging onto the server AND talking while muted on another account linked to your IP

Team Rules

1) ALL players using and/or raiding a base must be in the same Team.

2) Sharing information about a raid before the raid has started is disallowed. Sharing information about the raid after the raid has started is allowed.

3) Insiding and/or Merging after the Team Top Cutoff Period has been announced is disallowed.

4) A Disbanding Team cannot privately disclose Raid Information of their base; this information must be shared publicly in-game.

5) Accounts used for "alternative purposes" must be in the Team. Alternative Purposes include but are not limited to: AFKing inside a Base and/or Holding Items of Value within the Balance, Bank or player inventory

6) Accounts listed as a Team Alt cannot be used for "Gameplay Mechanics". Gameplay Mechanics include but are not limited to Gaining Skill, Raiding, Base Activities (i.e. Grinding, Farming) and/or Participating in Events

7) Any payments/trades of Items of Value between players in opposing Teams should follow a Common Sense Rule and be reasonable

8) Trapping and "Combat Tagging" a player within Claimed Territory in excess of 5 minutes without sufficient efforts to Kill the Player is disallowed.

Team Rule Definitions

  • Base refers to at least one of the following:

    • An underground area containing at least one of the following: Containers [Dispensers, Chests, Hoppers], Farms or Spawners
    • An aboveground area containing at least one of the following: Containers [Dispensers, Chests, Hoppers], Farms or Spawners

  • Raiding refers to:

    • Entering another Team's Base; and
    • Engaging in at least one of the following activities: Taking Excessive Items of Value from a Base, Destroying Walls/Floors/Ceilings in an "obvious" search for containers

  • Raid Information refers to at least one of the following:

    • The specific location/coordinates of the base

  • Insiding refers to at least one of the following:

    • One+ Team Member purposely revealing specific information about the location of their Team's value to another Team without the majority of their Team being "in the know"
    • One+ Team Member purposely distributing their value amongst another Team without the majority of their Team being "in the know"

  • Merging refers to:

    • A Team purposely trading, sharing, and/or giving a majority of their value to another Team, and
    • A majority of the players in one Team within the merger joining the second Team within the merger

  • Items of Value refers to at least one of the following:

    • Spawners, Wealth Shards, Utility Blocks of Value (i.e. Enchantment Table, Anvil, Grindstone), Excessive Sums of Currency
    • Excessive Sums of Money will be amounts greater than 1,000,000
    • Excessive Sums of XP will be amounts greater than 25,000

  • All Team-based rules are subject to manual review from Management

  • All Team-based rules take into account various bypasses, including but not limited to:

    • Revealing specific information about the value's location
    • Setting up fake bases/raids/containers to "look" legitimate
    • Leading players to a base/raid/chest with significant amounts of value

Team Rule Punishments

Breaking a Team Rule will result in 1 Team Strike! Each strike is equal to a 10% deduction of your value during the final calculations. Staff may give out a first offence warning at their discretion. Depending on the rule, these strikes may "follow" the Team Leader and Co-Leader or a lower Team Member.

Significant Additional Strikes: 5x Strikes

  • When a Team Member is punished for Evasion of a Significant Offence
  • Team Members may face personal punishments under the suspicion that they know and fail to report it
  • When a Team Member is punished for Duping

Additional Strikes: 3x Strikes

  • When a Team Member is punished for DDoS/Dox
  • When a Team Member is punished for Releasing Personal Information

Additional Strikes: 1x Strikes

  • When a Team Member is punished for Xray
