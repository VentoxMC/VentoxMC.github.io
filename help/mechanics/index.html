Gameplay Mechanics

Fishing

On Ventox, players can fish a variety of different landmarks, including the Shady Shallows in The Nexus. You cannot fish in the Raiding World.

Fishing Rods

Unlike normal Minecraft, Fishing is unique on Ventox. Players must obtain a custom Fishing Rod. There are 5x Types of Fishing Rods.

  • Simple Fishing Rod - 64 Uses - Buyable at landmarks and Spawn
  • Strong Fishing Rod - 128 Uses - Buyable at landmarks
  • Ultra Fishing Rod - 256 Uses - Obtained through Loot Tables
  • Master Fishing Rod - 512 Uses - Obtained through Loot Tables & Treasure Chests
  • Legendary Fishing Rod - 1,024 Uses - Obtained through Treasure Chests

Fishing Lake States

The Fishing Lakes have two different states: Overfished and Replenished. In either state, players can still fish.

In the Overfished state, which is the default state, players can find a variety of items including trash items, common and uncommon fish. Every few hours, the landmarks will naturally become Replenished. During this Replenished state, players can find a variety of items including trash items and fish ranging from uncommon to divine rarity. When the Shady Shallows is Replenished, players can also participate in an automatic weighted fishing competition that lasts for the entire replenishment. Players can also rarely fish out legendary Stock Rewards during a replenished state.

Players can purchase a Lake Replenisher or Pond Replenisher which, when activated, will instantly set the landmark's state to Replenished.

While Fishing at a landmark, players can find a variety of unique items.

Stock Rewards

When a landmark is in it's Replenished state, players have the chance to fish out a Stock Reward. A Stock Reward is a legendary item that has a limited number of stock within the fishing lake. This means that the item can only be fished out a certain amount of time before it's gone. At any given time, there is only a few Stock Rewards from the list that are actually available. The Stock Rewards will cycle and rotate through a season.

Some stock rewards have distinct uses including:

  • Random Supply Drop Summoner
  • Boss Summoner
  • Legendary Supply Crate
  • Trident
  • Legendary Treasure Chest

Some stock rewards can also be sold for big profits including:

  • Rainbow Clownfish
  • Enchanted Squid

Sea Monster

Players can summon a Sea Monster while using a Fishing Rod with Lure II or Lure III on the rod, while a landmark is in it's Replenished state. The Sea Monster is a random event that can occur, however it's quite rare.

When the Sea Monster is killed, it will drop a Wealth Shard but also a Stock Reward that cannot be obtained from fishing.

Schools of Fish

When a landmark is in it's Replenished state, there is a chance of "Schools of Fish" to spawn in. With this event, a random number of Fish Mobs will spawn in somewhere in the lake. These Fish Mobs can be killed to gain Fish Bait.

There are 3x Types of Schools of Fish.

  • Rare
  • Epic
  • Legendary

Weighted Fishing Competition

When a landmark is in it's Replenished state, there is a Fishing Competition going on. When you fish, the fish will be assigned a random weight. This weight helps determine the price of the fish, but will also contribute to the competition.

At the end of the replenishment, the Top Three Players with the heaviest fish will be given a reward.

  1. 2x Fisher's Bag
  2. 1x Fisher's Bag
  3. 1x Fisher's Lost Supplies

Fish Bait

Fish Bait is an item players can obtain through Schools of Fish that will increase the weight of fish when they're caught. Fish Bait can be very useful for having an advantage in the weighted fishing competition. Fish Bait is a single-use item.

There are 3x Types of Fish Bait

  • Rare - +5.0 Lbs
  • Epic - +15.0 Lbs
  • Legendary - +25.0 Lbs

Treasure Chests

Players can obtain Treasure Chests while fishing. Treasure Chests are rare items that are locked initially, requiring a Treasure Lockpick to unlock the chest. The Treasure Chests contain a variety of unique items, including the Lure and Luck of the Sea Enchantments.

Treasure Lockpicks

In order to open a Treasure Chest, players must obtain a Treasure Lockpick. Treasure Lockpicks can be obtained while fishing as well as through Gold Panning, Archeology and Voting.

Each Treasure Lockpick has a specified chance to work. Players can take the lockpick and drag-n-drop it onto a Treasure Chest. If the lockpick fails, the lockpick will break and the Treasure Chest will stay closed. If the lockpick is successful, the lockpick will still break, but the Treasure Chest will be opened.

Fishing Enchantments

There are 2x Fishing Enchantments. These enchantments must be applied to a Fishing Rod using an Anvil as Fishing Rods cannot be enchanted.

Lure

The Lure Fishing Enchantment is an exclusive Fishing Rod enchantment that is obtainable through through Treasure Chests. When a player is using a Fishing Rod with Lure II or Lure III, they have a chance to summon a Sea Monster.

Luck of the Sea

The Luck of the Sea Enchantment is an exclusive Fishing Rod enchantment that is obtained through Treasure Chests. When a player is using a Fishing Rod with Luck of the Sea, they have an increased chance to fish up a Stock Reward.

Luck Meter

When a landmark is in it's Replenished state, there is a server-wide Luck Meter. As players fish at the landmark, this luck meter will increase. The more players fishing concurrently, the high the luck meter will be. This luck meter improves the chance at finding Stock Rewards.

There are 4x Luck Levels.

  • Low - At least 0x Players Fishing
  • Medium - At least 15x Players Fishing
  • High - At least 30x Players Fishing
  • Divine - At least 50x Players Fishing

Each player fishing counts as 1x Player.

Players with the Trailblazer Rank count as 2x Players.

Players with the "Extra Luck" Skill Perk count as +1 Players.

Team members with the "+1 Luck" Summit Perk count as +1 Players.

Ice Pick

In the Christmas season, players can fish at the Snowdrift Shores. In order to fish, players must break the ice to reach the water and fish below. Ice Picks can be purchased from the Shop Merchants at the Snowdrift Shores during the speciality season.

Panning

On Ventox, players can pan at a designated gold deposit, including the Shady Deposits in The Nexus.

Gold Pans

Gold panning is a unique mechanic on Ventox. Before a player can gold pan, they must obtain a custom Gold Pan.

  • Gold Pan - 5 seconds - Buyable at landmarks and Spawn

Once a player has obtained a gold pan, they can navigate to a gold deposit such as the Shady Deposits. Once at the landmark, players can search for Rooted Dirt Blocks. Once these blocks have been identified a player can right-click on the block with the gold pan to "pan" the block. The player is unable to move while panning a block, so it does leave the player vulnerable.

Once the panning completes, the player will either receive an item or nothing. Unforunately, sometimes the panning action will not find any items in the blocks. In either situation, the Rooted Dirt Blocks will change into normal dirt blocks. After a random amount of time, the block will change back to a Rooted Dirt Block so it can be panned again.

Players can reduce the fail rate of Gold Pans with the "Reduce Fail Rate" Skill Perk.

Each panning operation will only find a single item, if it finds an item at all. This can be improved with the "Multifind" Skill Perk.

Gold Deposit States

The Gold Deposits have two different states: Overpanned and Restocked. In either state, players can still pan.

In the Overpanned state, which is the default state, players can find a variety of items including trash items, common and uncommon gold. Every few hours, the landmarks will naturally become Restocked. During this Restocked state, players can find a variety of items including trash items and gold ranging from uncommon to divine rarity. When the Shady Deposits is Replenished, players can also participate in an automatic weighted panning competition that lasts for the entire restockment.

Players can purchase a Deposit Restocker which, when activated, will instantly set the landmark's state to Restocked.

Mining

On Ventox, mining ores is very important to progress further in the game. Players are able to mine anywhere in the Raiding World or at a designated mineshaft. There are 2x designated mineshafts on Ventox including the Abandon Shafts mineshaft in The Nexus and the Hellfire Hollows mineshaft in The Nether Cavern.

As mining in the Raiding World is no different than in normal Minecraft, with the exception of a few Skill Perk requirements, the majority of this section will focus on the unique features of the mineshafts.

Elevator Operators

Abandon Shafts

In order to navigate between different layers of the Abandon Shafts, there are Elevator Operator NPCs scattered throughout the mineshaft. Upon reaching an Elevator Operator, players can right-click to commence a teleportation between the different layers of the mineshaft. During the teleportation, the player will glow leaving them vulnerable for discovery and attacks. Ensure you check your surrounding areas before using an Elevator Operator.

Hellfire Hollows

In order to navigate between different regions of the Hellfire Hollows, there are Gate Keeper NPCs scattered throughout the mineshaft. Upon reaching a Gate Keeper, players can right-click to commence a teleportation between the different regions of the mineshaft. During the teleportation, the player will glow leaving them vulnerable for discovery and attacks. Ensure you check your surrounding areas before using a Gate Keeper.

Mineshaft Layers

Abandon Shafts

The Abandon Shafts is split into 7x Unique Layers of ores.

  • Layer 1: Coal - Regeneration Rate of 45+ seconds
  • Layer 2: Coal, Iron - Regeneration Rate of 45+ seconds
  • Layer 3: Iron, Copper - Regeneration Rate of 60+ seconds
  • Layer 4: Copper, Gold, Redstone - Regeneration Rate of 60+ seconds
  • Layer 5: Gold, Redstone, Lapis - Regeneration Rate of 75+ seconds
  • Layer 6: Redstone, Lapis, Diamond - Regeneration Rate of 75+ seconds
  • Layer 7: Lapis, Diamond, Emerald - Regeneration Rate of 90+ seconds

Hellfire Hollows

The Hellfire Hollows is split into 2x Unique Regions of ores.

  • Region 1: Hellfire Hollow - Consists of Nether Quartz, Nether Gold and Glowstone Geodes
  • Region 2: Hellfire Hollow Depths - Consists of Nether Quartz, Nether Gold and Ancient Debris Spikes

Ore Regeneration

Abandon Shafts

In the Abandon Shafts, players can break any Ore Block as well as Cobblestone and Cobbled Deepslate. Once a block has been mined, it will turn to bedrock. After a random amount of time, the block will randomly change into one of those three blocks.

If the block is Cobblestone or Cobbled Deepslate, the regeneration is said to have "failed". In order for this block to regenerate, hopefully successfully, a player must destroy the block again and repeat this cycle. Players can purchase a Mineshaft Regenerator which, when activated, will instantly replace all the failed blocks with successful ores.

Once a day, the landmark will automatically regenerate all the failed blocks.

The fail rate can be reduced with the "Reduced Stone Rate" Skill Perk.

Hellfire Hollows

In the Hellfire Hollows, players can break any Ore Block as well as Stripped Mangrove Wood. Once a block has been mined, it will turn to bedrock. After a random amount of time, the block will randomly change into one of those three blocks.

If the block is Stripped Mangrove Wood, the regeneration is said to have "failed". In order for this block to regenerate, hopefully successfully, a player must destroy the block again and repeat this cycle. Players can purchase a Mineshaft Regenerator which, when activated, will instantly replace all the failed blocks with successful ores.

Once a day, the landmark will automatically regenerate all the failed blocks.

Mineshaft Refinery

The mineshafts have two different states for the "Material Refinery" that exists within the deep caverns: Inactive and Active. In either state, players can still fish.

In the Inactive state, which is the default state, players can mine Ore Blocks and Ore Blocks will regenerate. Once a day, the landmark's Material Refinery will become Active. During this Active state, players can mine Ore Blocks, however, Full Ore Blocks such as Diamond Blocks may regenerate, amongst the normal Ore Block regeneration. While the mineshaft is in an Active state, the block regeneration speed will also be faster.

Fossilized Chests

Players can obtain Fossilized Chests while mining, once they've acquired the proper Skill Perk. Fossilized Chests are rare items that are locked initially, requiring an Ancient Lockpick to unlock the chest. The Fossilized Chests contain a variety of unique items, including the Fortune IV Enchantments.

Ancient Lockpicks

In order to open a Fossilized Chest, players must obtain a Ancient Lockpick. Ancient Lockpicks can be obtained while doing Gold Panning, Archeology and Voting.

Each Ancient Lockpick has a specified chance to work. Players can take the lockpick and drag-n-drop it onto a Fossilized Chest. If the lockpick fails, the lockpick will break and the Fossilized Chest will stay closed. If the lockpick is successful, the lockpick will still break, but the Fossilized Chest will be opened.

Regeneration Meter

While at a mineshaft, there is a server-wide Regeneration Meter. As players mine at the landmark, this regeneration meter will increase. The more players mining concurrently, the high the regeneration meter will be. This regeneration meter improves the ore regeneration speed.

There are 4x Regeneration Levels.

  • Normal - At least 0x Players Mining
  • Enhanced - At least 15x Players Mining
  • High - At least 30x Players Mining
  • Divine - At least 50x Players Mining

Each player mining counts as 1x Player.

Players with the Trailblazer Rank count as 2x Players.

Players with the "Extra Regeneration" Skill Perk count as +1 Players.

Team members with the "+1 Regeneration" Summit Perk count as +1 Players.
