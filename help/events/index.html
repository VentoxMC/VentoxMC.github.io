Events

Summit

On Ventox, The Summit acts as an always active King of the Hill event.

The Summit can be in one of four states: Open, Capturing, Captured or Taking Over. Any player can view the current state of the The Summit using /summit.

  • /summits - Views the current state of The Summit.

When The Summit is in the Open state, any player can begin to capture The Summit. This is the default state whenever there is no active team on The Summit or if a team has not captured The Summit.

In order to participate in the event, players must be on a team that has a Team Core.

Capturing The Summit

When The Summit is in an Open state, any player can stand on the "Capture Point" located at the landmark. Once a player stands on the "capture point", their team will begin to capture the landmark. This will begin a 10 minute timer as well as make a global announcement. This sets the landmark into the Capturing state.

In order for the timer to continue, a member from the capturing team must remain on the "Capture Point" for the entire duration of the 10 minutes. If, at any point, all the team members leave the "Capture Point", it will stop the timer and reset it back to the beginning.

If the timer is timer is able to complete successfully, the capturing team will become the owners of The Summit and will receive 1x Summit Capture Reward. This will make a global announcement and sets the landmark into the Captured state.

Taking Over The Summit

When The Summit is in an Captured state, any player can stand on the "Capture Point" located at the landmark. Once a player stands on the "capture point", their team will begin to takeover the landmark. This will begin a 10 minute timer as well as make a global announcement. This sets the landmark into the Taking Over state.

In order for the timer to continue, a member from the capturing team must remain on the "Capture Point" for the entire duration of the 10 minutes. If, at any point, all the team members leave the "Capture Point", it will stop the timer and reset it back to the beginning. The original team that owned The Summit will remain as the owner.

If the timer is timer is able to complete successfully, the taking over team will become the owners of The Summit and will receive 1x Summit Capture Reward. This will make a global announcement and sets the landmark into the Captured state. The original owners of The Summit will lose their rewards.

Summit Rewards

When The Summit is captured, the capturing team will receive 1x Summit Capture Reward which will be placed into the Team Core Claim Rewards.

The owning team will also receive 1x Summit Holding Reward every 30 minutes that they hold The Summit.

The owning team will also receive 1x Summit Point which can be used for Summit Perks.

Summit Points

Summit Points are a virtual currency given to teams to purchase Summit Perks.

Losing The Summit

There are a few ways a team can lose control of The Summit.

If a takeover occurs, the original owner of The Summit will lose their ownership of the landmark and must conduct a takeover to regain control. In the process, the original owning team will lose ALL Summit Points and all Summit Perks.

If there is no activity on the "Capture Point" within 2 hours a team capturing The Summit, the ownership will be lost and the landmark's state will be set to Open. If there is any activity detected on the "Capture Point", this inactivity timer is reset back to 2 hours.
