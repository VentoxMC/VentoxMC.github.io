Quests & Missions

On Ventox, quests and missions are a series of activities that players can teams can embark on to receive some rewards and pass some time while on the server.

Quests

Quests are an always active activity that players and teams can engage with to receive some unqiue rewards.

There are two forms of quests: Daily Quests and Weekly Quests. As evident in the name, Daily Quests will reset every 24-hours and Weekly Quests will reset every 7-days.

There are two types of quests: Personal Quests, Team Quests. There are also Christmas season-exclusive Santa Quests.

Types of Quests

Quest Completion

Any player on Ventox can view the status of their personal quests or their team quests.

  • /quests - Views the progress of your quests.
  • /team quests - Views the progress of your team quests.

Players will receive a chat message when they have completed some or all of the required tasks for a given quest. Once this happens, players can travel to Spawn to find the Elite Quester, an NPC for Quests. When a quest has been completed, this is the only place that the rewards can be claimed at.

In the Christmas season, there are Santa Quests. Any player can view the status of their personal santa quests or their team santa quests.

  • /santaquests - Views the progress of your Santa quests.
  • /team santaquests - Views the progress of your team Santa quests.

Players must travel to Santa's Workshop to find Santa, an NPC for Santa Quests. When a Santa quest has been completed, this is the only place that the rewards can be claimed at.

Quest Rewards

Each quest is assigned a difficulty level ranging from Basic to Enhanced to Legendary. For each of these quest levels, players will be given a Quest Case equal to the difficulty level of the quest.

The Quest Cases guarantee money and some unique rewards. All team-based quests will also yield a Wealth Shard.

Quest Scrolls

By default, each player and team can only receive up to 3x Daily and 3x Weekly quests. Players may obtain Quest Scrolls which can be redeemed to increase the number of quests a player or team can have by 1. There is a maximum of up to 5x Daily and 5x Weekly quests.

Quest Scrolls can be obtained through Vote Boxes, Epic Loot Boxes and Team Boxes.

Missions

Missions are a sequential activity series for players to engage with to receive some unique rewards.

Unlike with the Quest System, missions must be completed in the order they are received to further your progression.

There is one type of mission: December Missions.

Types of Missions

December Missions

Throughout the month of December, players who log onto the server will be given a mission for each day of the month, up until the current day.

For example, logging in on December 8th will give you all the December Missions from December 1st to December 8th.
